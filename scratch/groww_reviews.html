  • Simple, Smart & Actually Helpful for Beginners
    13 Apr

    Direct Choko

    Ive been using Groww for a while now, and honestly, its one of the easiest investing apps Ive come across. The interface is super clean and not confusing at all, which really helped me when I was just starting out with mutual funds and stocks.What I like most is how straightforward everything feels — from KYC to making your first investment, the whole process is smooth and doesnt feel overwhelming. The app also provides basic explanations and insights, which are useful if you’re still learning the ropes.That said, theres still room for improvement. Sometimes the app can lag a bit during peak market hours, and I wish they had more advanced tools for experienced investors. But for beginners and even intermediate users, it does the job really well.Overall, Groww is a solid choice if you want a no-nonsense, user-friendly platform to start your investment journey without getting lost in complicated features.

  • Needs improvement
    04/06/2023

    Adi Oz

    I saw this app on the phone of a colleague and decided to download it as the UI looked clean and the options & calculators looked helpful. However, I encountered the same problem as any other Indian app which being, they rush to collect user data and get people to sign up without giving necessary information in the beginning. Main issue being, I am an NRI living in India currently and apparently this app doesn’t support NRI investing at the moment. This pop up - not really a pop up, it’s a small foot note which could have been easily missed or overlooked - only came after the app collected pretty much all KYC info like my PAN, email, phone number, Aadhar and now I am stuck at the signature screen. The most frustrating part is, there is no option to cancel the registration and delete my personal data or go back to main screen. I would be thankful if the developers can come up with a solution for this.

  • Utter chaos and ultimately loss making app
    14/04/2020

    Andhereismythought

    When I signed up, until I uploaded all the documents they kept reading me about it and once I uploaded they didn’t bother to do any KYC. But the dashboard didn’t show anything pending on my side. When I decided to checkout the app with a small amount, they waited until I transferred the money and then told me my ePAN cannot be accepted for KYC and kept my order pending. Note the NAV was around INR 27. Despite explaining on 2 calls they wouldn’t respect IT department directive that ePAN is equally valid. So I told them to refund the money, they replied we have initiated the refund and it will come back to account in 3 working days. Simultaneously another of their executive kept emailing me several times for me to email him the PAN scan. I didn’t bother to reply as my refund was in progress as per the Team Lead who spoke last. Separately 2 days later I found the PAN scan and uploaded it in the app. Now suddenly on Monday 3 days after my request for refund, I receive a note saying the money has been sent to the Mutual Fund and units will be allocated in 3 or 4 days. Finally the next day I receive a note saying I have been allocated x units @ NAV INR 29. Not only I had lost INR 2 per unit because of Groww’s mistakes and delays but also the cancellation that was initiated did not happen or they plainly lied to a customer. If this is how investors have to suffer at the hands of new age Brokers like Groww, I would rather desist from investing through such cheap apps. It’s funny their slogan is “proudly built in India for India.” They are apparently in business from 2016. Maybe that’s their level of professionalism. But they should drag the country’s reputation along with it. I humbly request them to change their slogan.
